COMPANY OVERVIEW

At Atlantic Express, we're a leading logistics company dedicated to providing tailored transportation solutions. Our comprehensive services include ground transportation, air freight, ocean freight, warehousing and distribution, and supply chain management.

OUR STRENGTHS

- Advanced Technology: We utilize Transportation Management Systems (TMS) and Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) to optimize logistics operations.

- Certifications and Compliance: We're committed to safety, quality, and compliance, holding certifications like ISO 9001:2015 and C-TPAT.

- Sustainability and Social Responsibility: We prioritize environmental sustainability, community involvement, and diversity and inclusion.

OUR RECOGNITION

We've earned recognition as a top logistics company, best place to work, and have received safety awards. Our goal is to deliver exceptional logistics services that meet the evolving needs of our customers.
